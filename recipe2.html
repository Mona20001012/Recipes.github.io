Creamy Chicken Penne Pasta

INGRIDIENTS

  • 4 slices bacon
  • 2 chicken breasts sliced
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 cloves garlic , minced
  • 2 cups spinach(80gm)
  • 4 small tamatoes,diced
  • 1 1/2 cups cream(360mL)
  • 1 cup shredded paramesan chesse(100g)
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes,plus more for garnish
  • 10 oz penne pasta(258g),cooked until aldente
  • 3 tablespoon Chopped fresh parsely for garnish

PREPARATION

  1. In a deep skillet fry the bacon until crispy. Remove to a papper towel to drain ,then chop
  2. In the same skillet,add the chicken and season with salt,papper,italian seasoning and paprika . Cook until no longer pink inside
  3. Add the garlic and cook until softended .
  4. Add spinach and tomatoes and cook until the spinach is wilted
  5. Add the cream ,paramesan ,an red papper flakes and bring to a boil
  6. Add the penne and bacon and stir until fully coated in sauce.
  7. Serve with parsley , oaramesan and red pepper flakes if desired
  8. Enjoy!
