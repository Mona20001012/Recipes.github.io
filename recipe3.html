Creamy Chicken Penne Pasta

INGREDIENTS

  • 1tbsp oil
  • ginger garlic paste as required
  • chopped toamtoes and salt
  • Red chilli powder , turmeric and funnel seeds

    • MARINATE(Optional)

    Marinate fish with red chilli powder ,turmeric ,very little salt and ginger garlic pasete.

    PREPARATION

    1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan . Add cumin and allow to splutter. The add onions and saute until golden.
    2. Add ginger garlic and fry for 30 to 60 seconds when they turn fragrant add tomatoes and half tsp salt.
    3. For until the raw smell goes away.
    4. Add red chilli powder , turmeric and funnel seeds(optional)
    5. Fry everything well until the onion masala smells good . Ensure the raw smell has gone . Cool this completely
    6. fry the merinate fish
    7. Heat a pan with 1 tsp oil, fry the fish until the raw smell of ginger garlic paste has gone . You can use the same pan used to fry the onions, i used a different one since used a steel pan . Set this aside
    8. Add the cooled ingredients to a blender jar along with 2 to 4 tbsp of water
    9. Blend to a coarse or smooth paste to suuit your taste . We prefer coarse texture in our curry so I made a coarse paste . If you prefer a smooth texture make a smooth paste.
    10. Add another tbsp. oil and heat on a low flame . When the oil turns slightly hot add curry leaves and green chillies
    11. When the leaves splutter,add the ground paste . Mix everything well
    12. Sprinkle garam masala and salt . You can add more red chilli powder at this stage to adjust the heat. I added a bit more.Fry everything well until the masala smells good.
    13. Pour 1 1/4 cup water and mix well to make lump free gravy. You may need to adjust the amount of water slightly
    14. On a medium flame boil until the oil begins to separate and the gravy thickens. This may take about 5mins.
    15. Check the salt and spice now . Adjust any taste that is needed . If you like to use tamarind, you can saok some tamarind in hot water
    16. Extract and filter the juice to the gray . We do not prefer tamarind so I have not used , the sourness from tomatoes is good enough
    17. Add the fish pieces to the gravy and cook for few mins . Flip them and cook until they are cooked completely . If desired garnish with coriander leaves.
